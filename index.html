Software ecosystem

Open hardware,
your software.

We don't sell software. Our hardware runs with the platforms your team already knows — and we connect you with partners who do the rest.

M

Mervis (IEC 61131-3)

Unipi's own SCADA and IDE platform — free with controllers. Full BACnet, Modbus, M-Bus, and IEC 60870-104 support.

NR

Node-RED

Flow-based rapid prototyping. Ideal for IoT integrations, edge logic, and quick deployments. Native API on all controllers.

HA

Home Assistant

Local-first smart building control. Perfect for residential and light commercial BMS without cloud dependency.

CS

CODESYS

Industry-standard IEC 61131-3 runtime on Edge and Patron controllers. Familiar for automation engineers.

D2

D2000 / IPESOFT

Industrial SCADA and MES for advanced process control and large-scale monitoring across manufacturing and utilities.

API

Custom & open API

Full REST API, MQTT, and Modbus TCP. Your own software, your own logic. We document everything openly.
